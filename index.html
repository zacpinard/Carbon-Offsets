Forest Carbon Offsets Map

California, Washington, and Quebec are currently the only states/provinces in North America with economy-wide carbon markets. This map displays a subset of the forest carbon offet projects registered in California's and Quebec's carbon markets. NDVI, a proxy for carbon content, is shown in each project's popup as a picture with darker shades of green indicating a higher NDVI gain from the date of registration to the same time of year in 2023.
